Goals/Mission

The goal of this website is to attract a younger audience to Slammers Beach Volleyball, increasing signups for people aged 15 to 30. The website will not only function as a means of advertising the club, but also facilitating online signups, streamlining and modernising the club's services - boosting the number of customers by at least 50%.

Target Audience

The site will be designed to appeal to a younger audience, specifically 15 to 30 year olds, as their current volleyball audience is 35 to 50 year olds. To target a younger, more tech-savvy age group, a modern, fresh website that meets current web standards will be produced. Living in a very visual age, images, colours and bold typography will be the focus of this website. Providing clear navigation, easy to access information, and plenty of visual stimulation.

Success Evaluation

The website will be deemed successful, and evaluated on the following information:

  • An increase of customers by at least 50%. Current regular players = 100, expecting at least 150 regulars now
  • More young families engaging in:
    • Volleyball (and professional competitions)
    • Socialising/attending social events
    • Use of the licensed bar & happy hours
  • More bookings for functions
  • A younger audience of players. Looking for 15 to 30 year olds, preferably high school students
  • More spectators at competitions
