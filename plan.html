Goals

To create a site that has:

  • Easy multipage navigation
  • Clear calls to action and invitations to get the user interested in volleyball
  • A clear layout and structure of information throughout the site
  • Strong and consistent branding
  • Emphasises a modern, clean and future-forward design
Most importantly, the site should produce results as specified in the success criteria

GitHub Repository

https://github.com/jhwebdesign/CP1406_1_JC428081_HILL

Success Evaluation

  • An increase of customers by at least 50%. Current regular players = 100, expecting at least 150 regulars now
  • More young families engaging in:
    • Volleyball (and professional competitions)
    • Socialising/attending social events
    • Use of the licensed bar & Wednesday happy hour
  • More bookings for functions
  • A younger audience of players. Looking for 15 to 30 year olds, preferably high school students
  • More spectators at competitions

Target Audience

The site will be designed to appeal to a younger audience. Current volleyball audience is 35 to 50 year olds, they are looking to target 15 to 30 year olds. A modern, fresh website that meets current web standards will need to be produced. Living in a very visual age, images, colours and typography should be bold and inviting.
